Connect Four Game!

The game starts with red piece assigned to player 1.

  • Add your piece by clicking on the top row.
  • The cell you click on will add piece to corresponding column.
  • The first one to play will be the player 1 [Red piece].
  • Take your turns alternatively.
  • The first play to match four pieces [horizontal/vertical/diagonal], wins!
  • Once done, click on Reset button to start again
